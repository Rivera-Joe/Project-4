Darryl and Shannon Medford were born and raised in Texas. They are the founders of Fisherman's Cove Lodge and also work to provide a fishing guide service by the name of Hooked Up Guide Service.
They have been residents of the Richland Chamber Lake area since 2011.
The Medfords enjoy traveling, and have added a few stamps to their passports along the way. Some of the places they have been are: The Bahamas, The Virgin Islands, California, Alaska and New York.
They enjoy volunteering, fishing and cooking, among other things.
